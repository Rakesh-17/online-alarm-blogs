Frequently Asked Questions

Why isn't my alarm working?

Make sure your browser tab stays open, your device volume is up, and you've allowed sound notifications. The alarm relies on your browser being active.

Can I use multiple timers at once?

Yes! You can set multiple alarms, timers, and use different tools simultaneously. Each tool operates independently.

Do you store my alarm settings?

Your settings are stored locally in your browser. We don't store personal alarm data on our servers. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Is Online Alarm free to use?

Yes, all our time tools are completely free to use with no registration required. We're supported by optional donations and non-intrusive advertising.

What browsers are supported?

Our tools work on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. For best results, keep your browser updated.

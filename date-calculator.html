What Is the Date Calculator?

Our comprehensive date calculator lets you perform three essential date calculations:

  • • Count Days: Calculate the exact number of days between two dates, with an option to include the end date.
  • • Add Days: Add a specific number of days to any date to find a future date.
  • • Subtract Days: Subtract a specific number of days from any date to find a past date.

How to Use the Date Calculator

📅

Calculate Date Difference

  1. Select Start Date: Click the first date picker to choose your starting date
  2. Select End Date: Click the second date picker to choose your ending date
  3. View Results: See the difference in years, months, days, and total days
  4. Detailed Breakdown: Get precise calculations including business days
  5. Copy Results: Click to copy the calculated difference to clipboard
➕

Add/Subtract Days

  1. Choose Base Date: Select your starting date using the date picker
  2. Enter Number: Type the number of days to add or subtract
  3. Select Operation: Choose to add (+) or subtract (-) days
  4. Get Result: See the calculated future or past date instantly
  5. Plan Ahead: Perfect for calculating deadlines and schedules
🎂

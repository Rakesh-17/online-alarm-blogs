How to Use the World Clock

🌆

Browse Popular Cities

  1. View Grid: Browse the grid of popular world cities
  2. Real-time Updates: See live time updates every second
  3. Time Zone Info: View UTC offset and local time for each city
  4. Add to Custom: Click any city to add it to your custom list
  5. Major Cities: Includes London, New York, Tokyo, Sydney, and more
⚙️

Add Custom Timezones

  1. Search Cities: Use the search box to find any city worldwide
  2. Select Timezone: Choose from the dropdown of available timezones
  3. Add to List: Click "Add Timezone" to add it to your personal list
  4. Remove Items: Click the X button to remove unwanted timezones
  5. Unlimited Cities: Add as many cities as you need
🗺️

Interactive World Map

  • Click Regions: Click on map regions to see timezone information
  • Visual Reference: See time zones laid out geographically
  • Day/Night Zones: Visual representation of day and night regions
  • Quick Selection: Fast way to find timezones by location
  • Educational Tool: Learn about global time zone distribution
